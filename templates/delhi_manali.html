Our Signature 4-Day Itinerary

Day 1: The Ascent

Arrival & Acclimatization

Overnight journey from Delhi. Check into a premium boutique stay in Kullu/Manali. Relax by the riverside, enjoy local authentic cuisine, and breathe in the fresh pine air.

Day 2: Solang Valley

Adventure Awaits

Head to Solang Valley for paragliding and snow activities. Evening stroll through Old Manali's vibrant cafes and heritage pathways.

Day 3: Local Heritage

Temples & Trails

Visit the sacred Hadimba Temple surrounded by cedar forests. Experience a curated heritage walk and explore hidden waterfalls off the tourist map.

Day 4: The Return

Kullu Shawls & Departure

Shop for authentic Kullu shawls and souvenirs. Begin the descent back to Delhi with unforgettable Himalayan memories.
