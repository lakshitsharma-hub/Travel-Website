Our Signature 4-Day Itinerary

Carefully crafted for a perfect blend of adventure, culture, and relaxation.

Day 1: The Ascent

Arrival & Riverside Retreat

After a scenic overnight or day drive from Delhi, you will enter the majestic Kullu Valley. Check into a handpicked premium boutique stay in Kullu or Manali. Spend your evening relaxing by the Beas river, enjoying a traditional Himachali dinner, and breathing in the fresh pine-scented air to acclimatize to the altitude.

Day 2: Peak Adventure

Solang Valley & Atal Tunnel

Get ready for high-altitude thrills. Head to Solang Valley for paragliding and snow activities. We will then drive through the engineering marvel, Atal Tunnel (Rohtang), to catch a glimpse of the stark, beautiful landscapes of Sissu in the Lahaul Valley. Return for a vibrant evening stroll through Old Manali's iconic cafes.

Day 3: Local Heritage

Temples, Trails & Naggar Castle

Immerse yourself in local culture. Visit the sacred wooden Hadimba Temple surrounded by ancient cedar forests. Later, take a curated heritage drive to Naggar Castle and the Roerich Art Gallery, experiencing the royal history of the Kullu region, ending the day by discovering hidden local waterfalls.

Day 4: The Return

Kullu Souvenirs & Departure

After a hearty breakfast, explore the local markets. Shop for authentic Kullu shawls, hand-woven caps, and local handicrafts. If time permits, we can take a quick detour for river rafting in Kullu before beginning the smooth descent back to Delhi with unforgettable Himalayan memories.

The Ultimate Road Guide & Pitstops

Everything you need to know before hitting the highway.

🛣️ Route Conditions

  • Delhi to Chandigarh (250 km): Smooth, flat NH44 highway. Fast moving traffic.
  • Chandigarh to Mandi (150 km): Four-lane mountain highway. Scenic but includes sharp curves.
  • Mandi to Kullu/Manali (130 km): The most beautiful stretch running parallel to the Beas River, though expect slower traffic due to hilly terrain.

☕ Best Pitstops

  • Murthal: Perfect for a heavy Paratha breakfast right after leaving Delhi.
  • Karnal/Ambala: Great for clean washrooms and coffee breaks (Haveli or Oasis).
  • Sundernagar Lake: A beautiful, quiet spot to stretch your legs before the final mountain climb.
