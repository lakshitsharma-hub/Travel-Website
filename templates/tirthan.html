Tirthan Valley: The Hidden Trout Paradise

May 2026 | By Local Experts

Agar aap Himachal mein commercial bheed (crowd) se door, ek shant aur sukoon bhari jagah dhoond rahe hain, toh Kullu district mein sthit Tirthan Valley aapke liye ek perfect escape hai. Neem-andhere pahaad, ghaney deodar ke jungle aur bilkul saaf behti Tirthan nadi is jagah ko jannat banati hain.

Kyun Khas Hai Tirthan Valley?

Tirthan Valley ki sabse badi khoobsurati ye hai ki ye abhi bhi bheed-bhaad se bachi hui hai. Ye valley UNESCO World Heritage Site — Great Himalayan National Park (GHNP) ka gateway hai. Agar aapko trekking ka shouk hai, toh yahan ke ghaney junglon mein eco-tourism aur wildlife spotting ka anubhav bemisaal hai.

Riverside Camping aur Trout Fishing

Yahan aane wale travelers ke liye sabse bada attraction hai nadi ke kinare camping karna. Raat ko taaron se bhara aasman aur nadi ki aawaz ke beech camp lagana ek alag hi sukoon deta hai. Iske sath hi, Tirthan ko India ka sabse bada Brown and Rainbow Trout Fishing spot mana jata hai. British zamane se hi ye jagah anglers ki favourite rahi hai.

Best Time to Visit Tirthan Valley

Tirthan Valley aane ka sabse behtar waqt March se June aur October se November ka hota hai. Spring ke mahino mein poori valley phoolon se ladi hoti hai, jabki Autumn mein pahaadon ka rang sunehra ho jata hai. Winters mein yahan halki barfbaari bhi dekhne ko milti hai.
