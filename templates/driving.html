5 Secrets to Master Mountain Driving

Himalayas ki khoobsurat vaadiyon mein road trip karna har traveler ka sapna hota hai. Lekin Shimla, Manali ya Spiti ki ghumaadaar sadoon par gadi chalana thoda challenging ho sakta hai. Agar aap pahaadon par safe aur smooth drive chahte hain, toh ye 5 local secrets aapke bahut kaam aayenge.

1. Engine Braking Ka Sahi Use (Never Coast in Neutral)

Dhalan (downhill) par chalte waqt kabhi bhi gadi ko neutral mein na dalein aur na hi lagatar brakes daba kar rakhein. Isse brakes garam hokar fail ho sakte hain. Hamesha chote gear (2nd ya 3rd) ka use karein, jisse engine khud gadi ki speed ko control karega (Engine Braking).

2. Climb Rules: Upar Aane Walon Ko Pehle Raasta Dein

Himalayan driving ka sabse bada niyam hai ki jo gaadi neeche se upar chadh rahi hai, use pehle raasta (right of way) milega. Upar chadhte waqt momentum ki zaroorat hoti hai, isliye agar aap downhill aa rahe hain toh apni gadi side karke unhe rasta dein.

3. Blind Turns Par Honking Hai Zaroori

Pahaadon par har mod ek blind turn hota hai jahan samne se aane wali gadi nahi dikhti. Isliye har acute curve aur hairpin bend par horn zaroor bajayein, taaki samne wale driver ko aapki presence ka pata chal sake.

4. Weather aur Landslide Alerts Check Karein

Himachal mein mausam tezi se badalta hai. Monsoons mein landslide zones aur winters mein black ice (sadoon par jami shaffaf barf) ka khatra rehta hai. Subah jaldi nikalna pahaadon par sabse safe mana jata hai kyunki raat ko visibility zero ho jati hai.

5. Overtaking ke Local Rules

Karan ya mod par kabhi bhi overtake na karein. Sirf tabhi overtake karein jab samne ki sadak bilkul saaf dikh rahi ho, aur hamesha aage chal rahi gadi se indicator ya pass ka signal milne ka intezar karein.
